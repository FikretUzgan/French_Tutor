## Key A1-A2 Grammar Topics (Standard Curriculum)

### CEFR A1.1 (Weeks 1-4)
**Grammar Topics:**
- Present tense: être, avoir, regular -er/-ir/-re verbs
- Articles (definite, indefinite, contractions)
- Gender and number agreement
- Basic adjectives placement
- Simple question formation (intonation, est-ce que)
- Negation (ne...pas)

**Vocabulary Target:** 500-700 words

**Example Topics:**
- Greetings & introductions
- Numbers 0-100
- Colors & basic descriptions
- Days of week, months
- Basic daily routines

---

### CEFR A1.2 (Weeks 5-8)
**Grammar Topics:**
- Passé composé (avoir, être)
- Agreement rules in passé composé
- Futur proche (near future: aller + infinitive)
- Imperative mood
- More complex adjectives (placement, agreement)
- Possessive adjectives (mon, ma, mes, etc.)

**Vocabulary Target:** 800-1200 words

**Example Topics:**
- Food & restaurants
- Family members
- Professions
- Simple past events
- Near future plans

---

### CEFR A2.1 (Weeks 9-12)
**Grammar Topics:**
- Imparfait (imperfect)
- Imparfait vs. Passé composé (when to use each)
- Futur simple (simple future)
- Comparatives and superlatives
- Relative pronouns (qui, que, où)
- Indirect objects & pronouns (lui, leur)

**Vocabulary Target:** 1200-1500 words

**Example Topics:**
- Travel & transportation
- Shopping & transactions
- Past narratives & stories
- Descriptions & comparisons
- Directions & locations

---

### CEFR A2.2 (Weeks 13-16)
**Grammar Topics:**
- Conditionnel présent (polite requests, hypothetical)
- Pronouns y/en (location/quantity)
- Reflexive verbs (se lever, se coucher, etc.)
- Indirect speech introduction (reported statements: "Il dit que...")
- Si-clauses (if sentences)
- Imperative with pronouns

**Vocabulary Target:** 1500-2000 words

**Example Topics:**
- Polite requests & wishes
- Conditional situations ("If I had...")
- Personal routines & habits
- Reported speech
- Problem-solving scenarios
