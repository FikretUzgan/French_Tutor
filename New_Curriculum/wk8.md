## Week 8 / Block 2 / CEFR Level A1.2

### Learning Outcomes (CEFR Descriptors)
- Can give simple commands, instructions and make polite requests using **impératif**.
- Can understand and follow basic written and oral instructions.
- Can combine impératif with object pronouns (me, te, nous, vous) in short sequences.

### Interaction Tier
**Tier:** 1  
**Interaction Type:** Reactive

### Grammar Target
**Form:** Impératif (formal & informal) + basic object pronouns  
**Complexity Rating:** 3/10  
**Prerequisite Knowledge:** -er, -ir, -re verbs present, y/en pronouns (light introduction)
**Scaffolding Steps:**
1. -er verbs: parle ! finis ! vends ! (tu form drops -s)
2. Être/Avoir: sois ! aie !
3. Formal (vous): parlez ! finissez !
4. With object pronouns: Donne-moi le livre ! → Donne-le-moi !
5. 6 example instructions: recipe, directions, meal preparation
6. Positive → negative: Ne parle pas ! N'y va pas !
7. Drill: Imperative sentences with 5 verbs (manger, prendre, aller, faire, dire)

### Vocabulary Set (21 words)
**Semantic Domain:** Instructions, Requests & Daily Commands  
prendre – mettre – ouvrir – fermer – venir – aller – faire – dire – donner – apporter – acheter – chercher – trouver – attendre – commencer – finir – vite – lentement – attention – s’il te plaît – merci

### Speaking Scenario
**Domain:** Giving Instructions & Requests  
**Prompt:**  
« Imagine que ton ami est chez toi. Donne-lui 6 instructions pour préparer un sandwich. Utilise l'impératif. »
**AI Role:** Follows along, corrects if there's misunderstanding: "You said 'take the bread', but which bread?"

### Reading/Listening Component
**Type:** Reading (short instruction text)
**Content:** 100-130 word recipe: "How to make iced tea" (step-by-step imperative)
**Task:** 5 questions (What is the first step? What do we add next?)

### Homework Assignment
**Type:** Text (Week 8 rotation: Text)  
**Task:**  
80–100 word text: "Write instructions for a friend to come to your place by metro."
Use at least 10 imperative sentences (positive + negative mix).
**Rubric:**  
- [ ] 10+ correct imperative forms
- [ ] Object pronoun or y/en used at least 2 times
- [ ] Instructions are clear and ordered
- [ ] High grammatical accuracy
- [ ] 80+ words
**Pass:** ≥70% (if not, imperative + pronoun remedial)

### Assessment Checkpoint
**Mini-Quiz (5 questions):
1. Write the imperative: Tu parles → … !
2. Translate: "Don't go there!" (negative imperative)
3. Complete with pronoun: Donne … (the book) to Marie. → Donne-le-lui
4. Transform to imperative: We finish now → … !
5. Build a sentence: "pay attention" + negative + imperative---
