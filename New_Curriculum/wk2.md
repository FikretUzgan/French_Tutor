# Week 2 / Block 1 / CEFR Level A1.1

**Theme:** Age, Nationality and Basic Numbers  
**Homework Rotation:** Audio (Week 2)

### Learning Outcomes (CEFR Descriptors – A1 Breakthrough)
- Can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance (e.g. very basic personal and family information).
- Can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple and direct exchange of information on familiar topics.
- Can describe in simple terms aspects of his/her background (age, nationality, where from).

### Interaction Tier
**Tier:** 1  
**Interaction Type:** Reactive (AI asks simple questions, student responds briefly, AI encourages and corrects)

### Grammar Target
**Form:** Avoir (present tense) + Expressing age with avoir  
**Complexity Rating:** 2/10  
**Prerequisite Knowledge:** Être from Week 1

**Scaffolding Steps:**
1. Explanation: "Avoir" means "to have". It is used for age: J'ai + number + ans = I am … years old.
2. Conjugation table:

| Pronoun   | Conjugation | English          |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| je        | ai          | I have           |
| tu        | as          | you have         |
| il/elle/on| a           | he/she/it has    |
| nous      | avons       | we have          |
| vous      | avez        | you have         |
| ils/elles | ont         | they have        |

3. Age structure: J'ai vingt-cinq ans. Tu as quel âge ?
4. Negative: Je n'ai pas trente ans.
5. Questions: Quel âge as-tu ? / Tu as quel âge ?
6. 6–8 realistic examples:
   - J'ai vingt-huit ans.
   - Tu as quel âge ?
   - Elle a dix-neuf ans.
   - Nous avons une maison à Istanbul.
   - Vous avez une grande famille ?
   - Ils ont vingt ans.

### Vocabulary Set (15 words)
**Semantic Domain:** Numbers, Age and Personal Information

1. un / une          – a / an  
2. deux              – two  
3. trois             – three  
4. quatre            – four  
5. cinq              – five  
6. six               – six  
7. sept              – seven  
8. huit              – eight  
9. neuf              – nine  
10. dix              – ten  
11. ans              – years (for age)  
12. âge              – age  
13. quel âge as-tu ? – how old are you?  
14. j'ai … ans       – I am … years old  
15. d'où viens-tu ?  – where are you from?

### Speaking Scenario
**Domain:** Sharing personal information  
**Example interaction:**

AI: Bonjour ! Comment t'appelles-tu ?  
Student: Je m'appelle Fikret.  
AI: Enchanté ! Quel âge as-tu ?  
Student: J'ai trente ans.  
AI: Très bien ! D'où viens-tu ?  
Student: Je viens de Turquie / d'Istanbul.  
AI: Parfait ! Tu as quel âge exactement ? (gentle repetition for practice)

### Reading/Listening Component
**Type:** Short text + slow audio  
**Text example (6–7 sentences):**

Bonjour, je m'appelle Sophie.  
J'ai vingt-deux ans.  
Je suis française.  
J'habite à Paris.  
J'ai un frère.  
Il a seize ans.  
Et toi ? Quel âge as-tu ?

**Listening task:**  
Listen and answer:  
1. How old is Sophie?  
2. Where does she live?  
3. How old is her brother?

### Homework Assignment
**Type:** Audio  
**Task:**  
Record a 40–60 second audio introducing yourself and one family member (name, age, where from, simple sentences).  
Example start:  
"Bonjour, je m'appelle … J'ai … ans. Je viens de … J'ai un/une … qui a … ans."

**Rubric (AI evaluation criteria):**
- [ ] At least 4–5 words from the set used correctly (numbers, age, avoir)  
- [ ] Avoir conjugated correctly at least twice  
- [ ] Age structure correct (number + ans)  
- [ ] Audio: clear pronunciation, natural pace  
**Pass Threshold:** 4/4 criteria met → proceed to next week  
**Failure Protocol:** Remedial loop – re-record with different ages/family members (AI generates new prompt)

### Assessment Checkpoint (Mini-Quiz – 5 questions)
1. Conjugate: Tu ___ quel âge ? → as  
2. Translate: How old are you? → Quel âge as-tu ?  
3. Complete: J'___ vingt-huit ___ . → ai … ans  
4. Make negative: Il a dix ans. → Il n'a pas dix ans.  
5. Listen and answer: In the audio, how old does the person say they are?
