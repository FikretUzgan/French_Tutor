# Week 4 / Block 1 / CEFR Level A1.1

**Theme:** Basic Daily Life, Time and Simple Questions  
**Homework Rotation:** Audio (Week 4)

### Learning Outcomes (CEFR Descriptors – A1 Breakthrough)
- Can understand very basic information about daily routines, time and simple personal questions.
- Can ask and answer simple questions, initiate and respond to simple statements in areas of immediate need or on very familiar topics.
- Can handle very short social exchanges using simple polite expressions and basic greetings/questions.

### Interaction Tier
**Tier:** 1  
**Interaction Type:** Reactive (AI poses simple questions about daily life/time, student responds briefly, AI encourages and corrects pronunciation/structure)

### Grammar Target
**Form:** Present tense regular -er verbs (basic: parler, aimer, habiter) + Yes/No and Wh- questions basics  
**Complexity Rating:** 3/10  
**Prerequisite Knowledge:** Être, avoir, articles from Weeks 1–3

**Scaffolding Steps:**
1. Explanation: Most French verbs ending in -er follow a simple pattern in present tense (like "to speak" = parler).
2. Conjugation table (using parler as model):

| Pronom   | Conjugaison     | English             |
|----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| je       | parle           | I speak             |
| tu       | parles          | you speak           |
| il/elle/on | parle       | he/she speaks       |
| nous     | parlons         | we speak            |
| vous     | parlez          | you speak           |
| ils/elles| parlent         | they speak          |

3. Other examples: aimer (to like), habiter (to live).
   - J'aime le français.
   - Tu habites à Istanbul ?
4. Yes/No questions: Est-ce que + verb → Est-ce que tu aimes le café ?
5. Wh-questions: Où habites-tu ? / Quel âge as-tu ? (recap + new)
6. 6–8 realistic examples:
   - Je parle français.
   - Tu aimes la musique ?
   - Il habite à Paris.
   - Nous aimons les enfants.
   - Vous parlez anglais ?
   - Elles habitent en Turquie.

### Vocabulary Set (15 words)
**Semantic Domain:** Daily life, time and preferences

1. parler             – to speak  
2. aimer              – to like  
3. habiter            – to live  
4. le français        – French (language)  
5. l'anglais          – English  
6. la musique         – music  
7. le café            – coffee

9. le matin           – morning  
10. l'après-midi      – afternoon  
11. le soir           – evening

15. j'habite          – I live

### Speaking Scenario
**Domain:** Daily routine and preferences  
**Example interaction (AI with student):**

AI: Bonjour ! Tu parles français ?  
Student: Oui, je parle un peu français.  
AI: Super ! Tu aimes le café ?  
Student: Oui, j'aime le café.  
AI: Et où habites-tu ?  
Student: J'habite à Istanbul.  
AI: Quelle heure est-il chez toi maintenant ?  
(AI praises, gently corrects verb endings/pronunciation, keeps it light)

### Reading/Listening Component
**Type:** Short text + slow audio recording  
**Text example (7–8 sentences):**

Bonjour, je m'appelle Lucas.  
J'ai vingt-quatre ans.  
J'habite à Lyon.  
Je parle français et anglais.  
J'aime la musique et le café.  
Le matin, je parle avec mes amis.  
Et toi ? Tu aimes le thé ?

**Listening task:**  
Listen and answer:  
1. Where does Lucas live?  
2. What languages does he speak?  
3. What does he like in the morning?

### Homework Assignment
**Type:** Audio  
**Task:**  
Record a 45–70 second audio introducing yourself + one daily preference/routine. Use at least 3 -er verbs (parler/aimer/habiter), time expression and 5+ words from the set.  
Example start:  
"Bonjour, je m'appelle ... J'habite à ... J'aime ..."

**Rubric (AI evaluation criteria):**
- [ ] At least 3 -er verbs used correctly (conjugation)  
- [ ] At least 5 words from vocabulary set used  
- [ ] Simple sentences understandable (structure & meaning)  
- [ ] Audio: clear pronunciation, natural pace (no rush)  
**Pass Threshold:** 4/4 criteria met → proceed to Block 2  
**Failure Protocol:** Remedial loop – AI generates new prompt (e.g. "Talk about what you like to do in the evening" with same verbs, different context)

### Assessment Checkpoint (Mini-Quiz – 5 questions)
1. Conjugate: Nous ___ français. → parlons  
2. Translate: I live in Istanbul. → J'habite à Istanbul.  
3. Question form: Tu aimes le thé ? → Est-ce que tu aimes le thé ?  
4. Fill in: Quelle ___ est-il ? → heure  
5. Listen and answer: In the audio, what time of day does the person mention?

**AI Note (for dynamic use):**  
If -er verb conjugations or question forms score <70% in weekly exam, remedial loop: Generate 6–8 new sentences with different -er verbs (manger, écouter, regarder) and mixed questions for targeted practice.---
