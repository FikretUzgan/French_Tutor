## Week 5 / Block 2 / CEFR Level A1.2

### Learning Outcomes (CEFR Descriptors)
- Can describe simple past events and actions using regular verbs in the **passé composé**.
- Can narrate a short personal story about a recent experience (e.g., what happened last weekend).
- Can understand and use basic time expressions related to the past.

### Interaction Tier
**Tier:** 1  
**Interaction Type:** Reactive

### Grammar Target
**Form:** Passé Composé with **avoir** (regular -er, -ir, -re verbs)  
**Complexity Rating:** 3/10  
**Prerequisite Knowledge:** Present tense conjugations, basic action verbs  
**Scaffolding Steps:**
1. Explain formula: **avoir** (présent) + participe passé  
   → j’ai parlé, tu as fini, il a vendu
2. Regular participles: -er → -é, -ir → -i, -re → -u
3. Show 6 example sentences (in daily life context)
4. Fill in the blank: Tu _____ (manger) une pizza hier ? → as mangé
5. Build sentences from prompts: "What did you do yesterday?" → 3-4 sentences
6. Error correction exercise (especially with avoir agreement issues)
7. Drill: Full conjugation of 3 verbs (parler, finir, vendre)

### Vocabulary Set (21 words)
**Semantic Domain:** Daily Activities & Recent Past  
manger – déjeuner – dîner – travailler – étudier – jouer – visiter – voyager – arriver – regarder – écouter – lire – acheter – vendre – payer – hier – la semaine dernière – le weekend passé – avant – après – pendant

### Speaking Scenario
**Domain:** Personal Experiences  
**Prompt:**  
« Raconte-moi ce que tu as fait hier soir. Utilise au moins 5 verbes au passé composé. »  
**AI Role:** Listener – asks follow-up questions: « Qu’est-ce que tu as mangé ? », « C’était bon ? »

### Reading/Listening Component
**Type:** Listening (short dialogue)
**Content:** 90-110 word audio: A friend describes what they did yesterday
**Task:** 5 comprehension questions (Who? When? What?)

### Homework Assignment
**Type:** Audio (Week 5 rotation: Audio)
**Task:**
30–60 second audio recording: "Describe your last weekend."
Use at least 8 words + 5 passé composé verbs.
**Rubric:**  
- [ ] 5+ correct passé composé verbs
- [ ] 8+ words used correctly
- [ ] Pronunciation is clear, natural pace
- [ ] Story is logically ordered
- [ ] Minimum 30 seconds, not reading from text
**Pass:** ≥70% (if not, remedial passé composé drill)

### Assessment Checkpoint
**Mini-Quiz (5 questions):**
1. Conjugue : Je (manger) une pizza hier.  
2. Traduis : « We watched a movie last night. »  
3. Correct: "Tu as fini tes devoirs hier?" (if correct, explain why)  
4. Which verb takes **avoir**? a) aller  b) parler  
5. Build a sentence: « visiter » + Paris + passé composé---
